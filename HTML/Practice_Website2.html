These are my top 3 thai Actors!

  • Pond Naravit
  • War Wanarat
  • Dew Jirawat

Pond Naravit

Naravit Lertratkosum nicknamed Pond is a Thai actor under GMMTV. He began his career when he won the third edition of Go On Girl & Guy Star Search by Clean & Clear back in 2020, he then signed an exclusive contract with GMMTV. He starred in several series such as: Fish Upon Sky, Never Let Me Go, We Are, Our Sky, Loneliness Society, Midnight Series: Dirty Laundry, Leap Day, etc


War Wanarat

War Wanarat Ratsameerat is a Thai actor who has so far mainly works in the Thai BL industry. He was part of a boy group project, but he started his own company with a friend (Anon Wong) and they run businesses and series which they earn millions of dollars from.


Dew Jirawat

Jirawat Sutivanichsak, known as Dew, is a Thai actor and model. He is artist popular for his acting debut in F4 Thailand: Boys Over Flowers in 2021, which gave him international fame. He is currently starring in the same series as Pond Naravit (Leap Day) which is gaining a lot of fame!
