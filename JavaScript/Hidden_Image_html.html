Is Gojo really dead?

Why people think he's actually dead:

  • A sudden cut to Gojo in the afterlife
  • His body being seen cut in half
  • Gojo's death was necessary for the plot to progress, allowing the next generation of sorcerers to step up and for the story to explore themes of loss and responsibility.

Why people think he's not actually dead:

  • Sukuna made the same mistake toji did which was not cutting his head off.
  • His brain was transferred into Yuta's body, and there's a possibility of his return.
  • The series ended without a definitive confirmation of Gojo's death, leaving his fate ambiguous
